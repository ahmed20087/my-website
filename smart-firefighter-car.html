Project Overview

1

Fire Detection

The flame sensor continuously monitors the environment for fire signatures. When a fire is detected, it sends signals to the Arduino Nano controller.

2

Autonomous Navigation

Arduino Nano processes sensor data and controls DC motors via L298N motor driver to navigate toward the fire source while avoiding obstacles.

3

Extinguishing System

Upon reaching the fire, the Arduino activates a relay that powers the water pump to extinguish the flames. A buzzer alerts nearby personnel.

4

Remote Control & Monitoring

HC-05 Bluetooth module enables wireless control via a custom Android/iOS application, allowing manual override and real-time monitoring.

Technical Components

Arduino Nano

The brain of the system - processes sensor data and controls all components with programmed logic.

DC Motors & L298N Driver

Two DC motors provide movement. L298N motor driver controls speed and direction based on Arduino signals.

Flame Sensor

Detects fire presence through infrared radiation. Highly sensitive with adjustable detection range.

HC-05 Bluetooth Module

Enables wireless communication between the car and smartphone app for remote control and monitoring.

Water Pump & Relay

12V water pump for extinguishing. 5V relay module safely controls high-voltage pump with low-voltage Arduino signals.

Buzzer

Audible alarm that activates during fire detection to alert nearby personnel.

Power Supply

Dual power system: 9V battery for Arduino/sensors, 12V battery for motors and water pump.

Control Application

Custom mobile app for manual control, real-time status monitoring, and emergency override functions.
